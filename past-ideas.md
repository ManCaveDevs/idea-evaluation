# Past Ideas — Evaluated & Shelved

Ideas we've already discussed, researched, and decided to pass on. Keeping them here so we don't revisit dead ends.

---

## 1. AI-Verified Accountability Reminder App ("ProveIt" Clone)

**Concept:** A reminder app that keeps ringing until you take a photo proving you completed the task (e.g., photo of recycling taken out), verified by AI.

**Why we passed:**
- Direct competitor **ProveIt** (useproveit.com) already exists with the exact same pitch — "locks your phone until you complete tasks with AI-verified photo proof"
- Softer competitors: Proofly (photo confirmation without AI), SnapRemind, PhotoMind
- iOS/Android restrictions severely limit persistent alarm / phone-locking behavior
- Accountability apps have high churn — people download in motivated moments, abandon when motivation fades

**Date evaluated:** March 27, 2026

---

## 2. FairPrice — Consumer Contractor Price Checker

**Concept:** Consumer-facing app where you describe a home repair job + ZIP code, and AI tells you the fair price range with labor/materials breakdown. Flip the pricing intelligence that contractors already have to the consumer side.

**Why we passed:**
- **Blueprint** (blueprinthq.app) is a consumer-facing app already doing this
- **BidCompareAI** exists in the same space
- **TheQwikFix** also overlaps
- ChatGPT itself can do rough price estimates, commoditizing the core value
- Built a full MVP and a v2 business brief before competitive analysis revealed the space was too crowded
- The "photo of a quote" angle was the only unowned differentiator, but not enough to justify entering

**Date evaluated:** March 27, 2026

## 3. AI-Verified Task Completion for B2B (Employee Verification)

**Concept:** Pivot of idea #1 to B2B — cleaning crews, landscaping, property managers take photos when work is done, AI verifies, business owner + client get proof.

**Why we passed:**
- Decent idea but deprioritized in favor of stronger opportunities
- ServiceTitan, Jobber, and other FSM tools are adjacent and could add this as a feature
- B2B sales cycle is slower — harder for a solo founder to get traction quickly
- Not killed outright, but ranked below other ideas with better market dynamics

**Date evaluated:** March 27, 2026

## 4. Tenant Move-Out Protection App

**Concept:** Walk through your apartment before moving out, take photos, AI assesses wear-and-tear vs. damage, generates a timestamped legal PDF to dispute deposit deductions.

**Why we passed:**
- One-time use per move — low retention and LTV
- Would need high volume or expansion into broader housing product to sustain revenue
- Ranked below other ideas with recurring revenue models

**Date evaluated:** March 27, 2026

---

## 5. Medication Adherence + Family Verification

**Concept:** Elderly person photographs pills, AI verifies they took them, family gets push notification confirmation or alert.

**Why we passed:**
- Healthcare-adjacent = regulatory complexity and high trust bar
- One wrong verification has serious consequences (liability risk)
- Highest potential but also highest risk of the original batch
- Deprioritized in favor of ideas with clearer paths for a solo engineer

**Date evaluated:** March 27, 2026
